About Me

I am a highly motivated and accomplished individual with a passion for computer science and mathematics. My journey began with notable achievements, including competing in Mexico's National Olympiads in 2017 and achieving recognition as the best computer science/math tutor of the year at PCCC in 2022.

My dedication to excellence is further demonstrated by my participation in prestigious competitions such as the Putnam competition, where I ranked in the top 500 in 2022. As the treasurer of the computer science club at my college, I successfully led a team in creating an innovative app focused on space, benefiting 400 students across the campus.

My coding journey was inspired by my father, who, with limited knowledge, created programming videos on YouTube. This inspiration fueled my commitment to learning and growing in the field. I honed my skills through programming courses from renowned institutions like Harvard and CourseCareers, preparing myself for a career in technology.

My practical experience was enriched through participation in HackPrinceton 2022/2023, where I thrived in a fast-paced environment, delivering impactful projects within tight timelines. I am driven by a passion for continuous learning, problem-solving, and contributing meaningfully to the world of technology.

As I reflect on my achievements and experiences, my long-term goal crystallizes around a deep passion for leveraging technology to benefit the Hispanic community. Looking ahead, I am driven by the vision of creating my own startup, utilizing artificial intelligence to address challenges and empower individuals within the Hispanic community. I envision developing innovative solutions that contribute to education, healthcare, or other areas where technology can make a tangible difference. This goal not only fuels my entrepreneurial spirit but also underscores my commitment to creating positive social impact through technology.

Looking forward, I am excited to leverage my skills and experiences to tackle new challenges and make a positive impact in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
